# Cycura_app
A large number of women experience PCOD, PCOS, irregular periods, and severe menstrual symptoms, but they lack proper awareness, personalized guidance, and affordable support to manage these conditions.

Polycystic Ovaryian Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder affecting women, characterized by irregular menstrual cycles, excess androgen levels, and often the presence of cysts on the ovaries.
It can lead to: -
Infertility,Irregular periods,No ovulation,Cardiovascular Diseases,Liver inflammation,Persistent acne,Excess facial and body hair,Hair thinning,Insulin resistance,Rapid weight gain,Type 2 Diabetes,Mood swings, Chronic depression, anxiety disorders,Higher risk of sleep disorders
If left untreated and undiagnosed it may even lead to endometrial or uterine cancer
PCOD side effects are not isolated—they form a cascade:
Hormonal imbalance → metabolic disruption → physical symptoms → mental stress → long-term disease risk


A personalized, AI-assisted menstrual wellness platform that integrates menstrual health, mental well-being, lifestyle guidance and sustainability.
What we offer?
1.	A smart period tracker
That tracks your cycle length and irregularities, further based on user lifestyle data asked in form of questionnaire while login in the app, daily logs and special logs of various physical and mental health symptoms in the body overtime
A ML model will: -
Predict the next cycle
Pcod/Pcos risk detection
It’s based on data collection, pattern analysis and adaptive learning.
It also personalizes the users’ experience by asking a set of questions on a regular basis, and specific questions according to the phases in the cycle. So that user receive phase-based insights about physical and mental health.
2.	Personalised Health care Recommendations
Cycura delivers personalized lifestyle recommendations, including diet plans, exercise routines, and skincare guidance, tailored to each phase of the user’s menstrual cycle. This ensures that users receive phase-specific support to manage hormonal changes effectively and improve overall well-being.
3.	Therapist support
In-app access to certified therapists specializing in PCOD/PCOS and menstrual health related mental wellbeing.
4.	Mental Health Integration
The app provides customized AI-generated playlists based on User’s mood and the menstrual phase; it helps to reduce stress, anxiety, and emotional fatigue.
Mental health during menstruation is often ignored but we bring it to the center.
5.	24×7 AI Chatbot
The app provides a feature to answer all the doubts in a judgement freeway optimizing user’s experience.
6.	Awareness & Education Section
The app includes a dedicated Awareness & Education section that provides simple, accurate, and stigma-free menstrual health knowledge. (Our mission is to not only ease the menstruation related problems of women but also create awareness about pcod and normalizing talks over period and reproductive health).
7.	Myth Breaker Section
It debunks common menstrual myths using scientifically verified and easy-to-understand explanations.
